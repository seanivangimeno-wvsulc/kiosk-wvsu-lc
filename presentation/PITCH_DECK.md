# Project Presentation Slide Deck Outline

This file contains the slides layout and presenter notes for pitching the WVSU-LC Kiosk project to stakeholders and school administrators.

---

## 📽️ Slide 1: Title & Vision
* **Title**: WVSU-LC Interactive Campus Kiosk
* **Subtitle**: Streamlining Campus Information & Navigation
* **Presenter**: Sean Ivan Gimeno
* **Visual**: Render of the homepage UI on a mockup touchscreen stand.

## 📽️ Slide 2: The Problem
* **Bullet Points**:
  - Visitors and freshmen struggling to find classrooms in new buildings.
  - Printed campus maps and directories become outdated rapidly.
  - School announcements are scattered across different social channels and noticeboards.
* **Presenter Note**: Explain the daily friction students and visitors experience looking for offices.

## 📽️ Slide 3: The Solution
* **Bullet Points**:
  - Interactive, touch-optimized SVG Campus Map.
  - Live faculty & office search directory.
  - Integrated digital bulletin board for real-time announcements.
* **Presenter Note**: Emphasize how this is a unified, accessible digital concierge.

## 📽️ Slide 4: Technology Stack
* **Bullet Points**:
  - **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Motion (smooth animations).
  - **Backend**: Express server with API integrations.
  - **Hardware**: Dedicated touch terminals configured in kiosk mode.
